STATUS: ⚠️ WARNING - Progressive Moisture Contamination

===============================================================================
                      PROBLEM SUMMARY
===============================================================================

Transformer TR-004 is experiencing progressive moisture contamination due to 
a malfunctioning silica gel breather system. The moisture content has risen 
from acceptable levels (12 ppm in 2020) to critical levels (48 ppm currently).

ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS:
1. Primary: Silica gel breather saturation (identified Q2 2023)
2. Secondary: Suspected gasket degradation at main tank cover
3. Contributing: Thermal cycling creating moisture migration

===============================================================================
                   IMPACT ASSESSMENT
===============================================================================

1. INSULATION DEGRADATION
   - Paper insulation moisture absorption
   - Accelerated cellulose aging
   - Reduced dielectric strength
   
2. OIL QUALITY DETERIORATION
   - BDV dropped from 68 kV to 35 kV (48% reduction)
   - Increased particle contamination
   - Reduced interfacial tension

3. OPERATIONAL RISKS
   - Reduced overload capability
   - Lower insulation margin
   - Risk of flashover during surge events

1. Replace silica gel breather - PRIORITY HIGH
2. Perform visual inspection of all gaskets
3. Schedule vacuum dehydration treatment

MEDIUM-TERM (Within 60 days):
1. Complete gasket survey and replacement
2. Hot oil circulation treatment
3. Install online moisture monitoring
